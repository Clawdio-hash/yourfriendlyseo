Google also dropped a spam update March 24-25. Unusually fast rollout. If you saw sudden drops around then, check your backlinks and content quality.

Brutal stat: Only 1% of AI Overview searches result in a click. 60% of all searches now end without a click.

What to do: Target queries AI can't fully answer. Build your brand so people search for YOU.

"Set and forget" doesn't work anymore. Businesses posting weekly to GBP are winning local rankings. Treat it like social media.

**5. E-E-A-T is everything**

Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness. Google's getting better at spotting generic content vs. real expertise. Show your credentials.
