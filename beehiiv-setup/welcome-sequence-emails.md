# Beehiiv Welcome Sequence - Email Copy

## Dashboard Setup Instructions

### Step 1: Access Automations
1. Log into Beehiiv → Go to **Automations** (left sidebar)
2. You have a draft automation called "New Automation" — you can edit this or create a new one

### Step 2: Create Welcome Sequence
1. Click **Create Automation** (or edit existing)
2. Name it: "Welcome Sequence"
3. Set trigger: **When someone subscribes** (signup)

### Step 3: Add Emails
Add these 3 actions in sequence:

---

## Email 2: Quick Win (Day 3)

**Subject:** The one SEO fix that takes 10 minutes and actually works

**Preview Text:** I've seen this boost rankings in 2 weeks

---

Hey,

Quick tip today — something you can do in the next 10 minutes that might actually move the needle.

**Go update your title tags.**

Seriously. It's that simple.

Here's the formula I've used for years:

`[Primary Keyword] - [Benefit/Context] | [Brand Name]`

Example: "DIY SEO Guide - Rank Your Small Business Website | Your Friendly SEO"

Why this works:
- Primary keyword up front = Google knows what the page is about
- Benefit/context = humans know why they should click
- Brand name = builds recognition over time

**Your 10-minute action plan:**

1. Open Google Search Console
2. Find your top 10 pages by impressions
3. Check if the title tags follow this formula
4. Update the ones that don't
5. Wait 2 weeks and check your CTR

I've seen this single change increase click-through rates by 15-30%. Not guaranteed, but it's free and takes 10 minutes. Worth a shot.
