## 🎯 PHASE 1: Foundation (Months 1-3)

### Targets
- **Sessions:** 500/month by Month 3
- **Revenue:** First sale within 30 days
- **Email Subscribers:** 100 by Month 3
- **Content:** Minimum 2 posts per week
- **Technical:** All posts indexed in Google Search Console within 2 weeks of publishing

### Success Criteria
- Establish content publishing rhythm
- Validate product-market fit with first sale
- Build initial email list
- Ensure technical SEO foundation is solid

- **Sessions:** 5,000/month by Month 6

- **Email Subscribers:** 500 by Month 6
- **Rankings:** 5 keywords on page 1 of Google

### Success Criteria

- Consistent monthly revenue

- Page 1 rankings for target keywords

## 📊 Weekly Reporting (Every Monday)

**Report via Telegram including:**

1. **Sessions vs Goal**
   - Current month sessions to date
   - Weekly growth %
   - On track vs behind

2. **Revenue vs Goal**
   - Current month revenue
   - Products sold
   - Conversion rate

3. **Top Ranking Keywords**
   - Current positions
   - Movement (up/down)
   - Impressions & clicks

4. **Content Published**
   - Posts published last week
   - Posts planned this week
   - Content gaps identified

5. **What's Working**
   - Top traffic sources
   - Best performing content
   - Winning strategies

6. **What Needs Adjustment**
   - Underperforming areas
   - Blockers
   - Action plan for next week

---

## 🚨 Immediate Alerts

**Flag via Telegram immediately if:**

- Sessions trending >20% below target
- Revenue >30 days behind first-sale goal
- Content publishing falls below 2 posts/week for 2 consecutive weeks
- Technical SEO issue preventing indexing
- Major traffic drop (>30% week-over-week)

- **Email platform:** Subscriber growth, open rates, click rates
